BoboSlicer is designed to work with implicit function representations, providing efficient and accurate slicing for DLP 3D printing systems. It handles complex geometries and enables high-resolution fabrication without the need for mesh conversion.

### Key Features

- **Implicit representations**: Works directly with implicit functions, avoiding mesh conversion errors
- **High resolution**: Enables precise DLP printing with fine layer details
- **Efficient processing**: Optimized algorithms for large-scale models and complex geometries
- **Multi-axis support**: Compatible with advanced printing setups and curved layer generation
