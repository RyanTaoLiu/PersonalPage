This collaborative project with **Broetje-Automation GmbH** and **Airbus** focuses on developing a vector-field guided tool-path planning strategy for 3D printing with continuous carbon fiber (CCF).

### Key Goals

- Design vector-field based tool-paths for CCF that respect manufacturing constraints
- Improve structural performance and print quality for industrial 3D printing setups
- Enable robust, automation-friendly workflows for large-scale composite fabrication

### Status

- **Completed**
